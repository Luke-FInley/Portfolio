SPLAT
As of April 2025, SPLAT is one of the most played songs by the artist Connor Price. This is my take on a music video for the song!

This project was born from a class I took in the Fall of 2024. That class taught us how to utilize Adobe Premiere Pro along with a few other softwares, however, this project was made using only Adobe Premiere Pro.
The Process
First Steps
SPLAT was the first project I had put serious time and effort into when it came to using Premiere Pro. I had messed around with its tools a couple times prior, but never took anything serious before SPLAT. It didn't take me long to really latch on to the project. I love learning new skills and this project was a perfect catalyst to satiate that want.
