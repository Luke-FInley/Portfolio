Steamore
Steamore is the name given to this 3D printed dragon head that produces vapor from its nostrils. The head of the creature is Alduinâ€™s from the 2011 game, Skyrim. This project came from a class I took in the Fall of 2023 that taught us the basics of the Arduino IDE and hardware.

I got the idea for this project when I was playing D&D (Dungeons and Dragons). I have always loved physical props for the game (see the Final Showdown projects in my portfolio for more proof of that) and so I thought about what kind of tech could I bring to the table for a prop? Thus Steamore was born.
